description: "OptSusp is an open-source computational framework for the analysis and optimization of passive vehicle suspension systems. The software provides MATLAB-based implementations for quarter-car modeling, asymmetric damping analysis, ride-comfort evaluation, road-holding assessment, and stochastic optimization of suspension parameters. Designed for research in vehicle dynamics and vibration control, the framework enables scenario-driven optimization under different road and excitation conditions, supporting studies on comfort–safety trade-offs and suspension performance."
